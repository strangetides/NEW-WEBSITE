The Game of DODECA

Discover an endless world of geometry

Embark intellectual voyage where art and strategy collide!

Welcome to DODECA, an enthralling voyage into the world of sacred geometry and tactical gameplay. Enter a world where everything you do is calculated to exact heavenly proportions. In DODECA, you are invited to explore the symmetry beauties of geometric shapes and find mystical axis points by charting your path along holy lines.

Test your intellect by solving the geometric patterns and moving along the lines wisely to reveal the mysteries that are concealed behind each axis point. A distinctive gaming experience, DODECA combines the excitement of strategic decision-making with the beauty of sacred geometry.
